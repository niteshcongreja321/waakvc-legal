Waak VC Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

Waak VC ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application that helps you track voluntary contributions (VCs), payments, profits, and related financial records. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you when you use the Waak VC app ("App").

Questions about this policy? Contact niteshcongreja321@gmail.com.

1. Who we are

Waak VC is operated by Nitesh Kumar. The App lets you create an account, record VCs and payment entries, view dashboards and reports, change your preferred currency, and use an in-app VC calculator. The App does not move real money or connect to bank accounts.

2. Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to provide the App:

  • Account information: email address, password (stored in hashed form by our authentication provider), and optional name details you provide at sign-up or in your profile.
  • Authentication data: if you choose Google or Sign in with Apple, we receive account identifiers and profile details made available by those providers through our authentication service.
  • Financial information you enter: VC titles, amounts, currencies, participant counts, interest rates, payment dates, amounts paid, profit calculations, and related notes or descriptions.
  • App preferences: your selected main currency and similar settings stored for your account or device.
  • Feedback you send: if you use the in-app feedback form, we receive the message content and basic context you submit (such as your email and selected currency).

3. How we use information

We use your information to:

  • Create, authenticate, and manage your account
  • Store and sync your VC and payment data
  • Display dashboards, reports, and calculator results
  • Send account-related emails such as verification or password reset links
  • Respond to support and feedback requests
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking SDKs in the App.

4. Where data is stored

Your account and synced VC data are stored using Supabase, our cloud service provider for authentication and database hosting.

Feedback messages submitted through the App may be delivered using EmailJS so we can receive your message by email.

Some preferences may also be cached locally on your device for performance and offline resilience.

5. Data sharing

We share information only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers: with Supabase for authentication and data storage, and with EmailJS when you submit feedback.
  • Sign-in providers: if you use Google or Apple sign-in, authentication is handled according to those providers' policies.
  • Legal requirements: if required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

6. Data retention

We keep your account and VC data while your account remains active. You can delete your account in the App, which permanently removes your account and associated VC and payment records from our systems.

Local data cached on your device may remain until you uninstall the App or clear the App's storage from your device settings.

7. Your choices and rights

You can:

  • Update your profile information inside the App
  • Change your preferred currency in Settings
  • Sign out of your account
  • Delete your account and associated data from the Account screen
  • Contact us with privacy questions or requests

8. Security

We use account authentication, access controls, and encrypted network connections to protect your data. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children

Waak VC is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International users

Your information may be processed and stored in countries where our service providers operate.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: niteshcongreja321@gmail.com
