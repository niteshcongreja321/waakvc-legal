Waak VC — Delete Your Data

Effective date: June 30, 2026

This page explains how to delete specific data stored in Waak VC without deleting your account. Waak VC is operated by Nitesh Kumar.

If you want to delete your entire account instead, see Delete your account.

Delete data in the app

You can remove individual records directly inside the Waak VC app. You must be signed in. Your account will remain active.

Delete a VC and its payments

  1. Open the Waak VC app and sign in.
  2. Open the VC you want to remove.
  3. Tap the delete option for that VC.
  4. Confirm when prompted.

This permanently deletes that VC and all payment records linked to it.

Delete a single payment

  1. Open the Waak VC app and sign in.
  2. Open the VC that contains the payment.
  3. Open the payment you want to remove.
  4. Choose Delete payment and confirm.

The VC and your other payments will remain.

Remove “VC taken” information

  1. Open the Waak VC app and sign in.
  2. Open the relevant VC.
  3. Edit the VC taken details.
  4. Choose Remove to clear that information.

The VC itself and its payment history will remain unless you delete them separately.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, or you want us to remove data that cannot be deleted in the app (such as a past feedback message), email us from the address linked to your Waak VC account:

niteshcongreja321@gmail.com

Include:

  • Subject line: Waak VC data deletion request
  • The email address used for your Waak VC account
  • What data you want deleted (for example, a specific VC name, payment, or feedback message)

We will verify ownership and complete the request within 30 days.

Data you can delete without closing your account

  • Individual VC records (titles, amounts, currencies, notes, and related details)
  • Individual payment records (dates, amounts paid, and related details)
  • VC taken information attached to a VC
  • Past feedback or support messages (by email request)

Data that is kept unless you delete your account

  • Your account email, name, and sign-in credentials
  • App settings such as preferred currency, theme, and reminder preferences
  • Any VCs or payments you do not delete yourself

To remove your account and all remaining data, use Delete your account.

Retention period

Data you delete in the app is removed from our active systems immediately once the deletion syncs. Email deletion requests are completed within 30 days.

Local copies cached on your device may remain until you reinstall the app or clear the app's storage in your device settings.
