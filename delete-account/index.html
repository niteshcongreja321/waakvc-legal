Waak VC — Delete Your Account

Effective date: June 30, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Waak VC account and the data associated with it. Waak VC is operated by Nitesh Kumar.

Delete your account in the app (recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account is directly inside the Waak VC app. You must be signed in.

  1. Open the Waak VC app on your device.
  2. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
  3. Go to the Account tab.
  4. Scroll to the Account section.
  5. Tap Delete account.
  6. Type DELETE to confirm.
  7. Tap Delete my account.
This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email us from the address linked to your Waak VC account:

niteshcongreja321@gmail.com

Include:

  • Subject line: Waak VC account deletion request
  • The email address used for your Waak VC account
  • A clear statement that you want your account and associated data deleted

We will verify ownership of the account and complete the deletion within 30 days.

Data that is deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • Your account credentials and profile (email, name, and sign-in metadata)
  • All VC records you created (titles, amounts, currencies, notes, and related details)
  • All payment records linked to your account
  • Your synced app settings (such as preferred currency and theme preferences)
  • Your authentication record from our database

Data that may be kept

  • Feedback or support messages you previously sent by email or through the in-app feedback form may remain in our support mailbox until we manually remove them. These are not tied to your active account after deletion.
  • Local data on your device (cached records and preferences) may remain until you uninstall the app or clear the app's storage in your device settings.
  • Service-provider backups or logs maintained by our hosting and authentication providers may be retained for a limited period according to their policies, then removed automatically.

Retention period

Account and associated VC data are deleted from our active systems immediately when you delete your account in the app, or within 30 days of a verified email deletion request. We do not retain deleted account data for ongoing use.

Delete some data without deleting your account

You can remove individual VC and payment records inside the app without closing your account. Open a VC, then use the delete options for that VC or its payments.
